## Section 2: Use Cases (The Triad)

### Layout
- **Cards**: Minimalist, secure, "Vault" aesthetic.
- **Style**: Using `border-emerald-500` for "Verified/Safe" accents.

### 1. HFT & Market Abuse
- **Goal**: Prevent spoofing/layering in milliseconds.
- **Axiara Role**: Real-time surveillance of trading algorithms to ensure market integrity.

### 2. Fair Lending (Bias Audit)
- **Goal**: Compliance with ECOA (Equal Credit Opportunity Act).
- **Axiara Role**: Logic mapping to prove credit decisions are race/gender-neutral.

### 3. Fraud Detection
- **Goal**: Reduce false positives (insult rate).
- **Axiara Role**: Precision governance of anomaly detection models.
