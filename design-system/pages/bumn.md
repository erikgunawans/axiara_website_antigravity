## Section 2: Use Cases (The Triad)

### Layout
- **Cards**: Structural, clear, "Brutalist/Official" aesthetic.
- **Style**: Focus on stability and trust.

### 1. Satu Data Integration
- **Goal**: Unified data governance across silos.
- **Axiara Role**: Automated schema mapping and lineage tracking for "Satu Data Indonesia".

### 2. Public Service Algorithmic Audit
- **Goal**: Fairness in citizen services (e.g., subsidies, logistics).
- **Axiara Role**: Bias testing for equitable distribution algorithms.

### 3. Infrastructure Resilience
- **Goal**: National Cyber Resilience (BSSN alignment).
- **Axiara Role**: Continuous red-teaming for Critical Information Infrastructure (CII).
