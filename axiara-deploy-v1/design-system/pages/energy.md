## Section 2: Use Cases (The Triad)

### Layout
- **Cards**: 3 distinct cards with "Energy" specific iconography.
- **Style**: Robust, industrial, clean.

### 1. Smart Grid Load Balancing
- **Goal**: Optimize distribution in real-time.
- **Axiara Role**: Audit algorithms managing renewable integration to prevent blackouts.

### 2. Predictive Asset Maintenance
- **Goal**: Prevent turbine/transformer failure.
- **Axiara Role**: Validate sensor data integrity and maintenance prediction models.

### 3. Carbon Credit Verification
- **Goal**: Automated ESG reporting.
- **Axiara Role**: Immutable audit trails for carbon offset calculations.
