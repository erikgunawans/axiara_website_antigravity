Why ISO 42001?

Trust is no longer abstract.
It is certified.

ISO/IEC 42001 is the first global standard specifying requirements for establishing, implementing, maintaining, and continually improving an Artificial Intelligence Management System (AIMS). It provides the verifiable proof that your organization governs AI responsibly.

PLAN

Context & Leadership (Clauses 4-5)

Define the scope of your AIMS. Establish the AI Policy, assign roles and responsibilities, and ensure top management commitment to responsible AI.

DO

Planning & Support (Clauses 6-7)

Conduct AI risk assessments (impact analysis). Ensure resources, competence, awareness, and documented information are available for the AIMS.

CHECK

Operation (Clause 8)

Operational planning and control. Manage the AI system lifecycle, from design and development to deployment and monitoring.

ACT

Performance & Improvement (9-10)

Internal audit and management review. Monitor, measure, analyze, and evaluate the AIMS performance. Take corrective actions.
