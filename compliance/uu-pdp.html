Accountability & Notification

3x24 Hours To Report.

Under UU PDP, data controllers must notify the authority and data subjects within **72 hours** of a breach. Failure to comply can result in administrative fines up to 2% of annual revenue or criminal sanctions.

Data Controller Obligations

Lawful Basis

Ensure explicit consent or legitimate interest exists for every processing activity.

Purpose Limitation

Process data *only* for specific, explicitly stated purposes communicated to the subject.

Security Measures

Implement technical and measures (encryption, access control) to prevent unauthorized access.

Breach Notification

Mandatory written notification to subjects and authorities within 3x24 hours of failure.

Data Subject Rights

Data subjects have the right to request a copy of their personal data and understand how it is being processed. Organizations must respond within the statutory period.
Subjects can request rectification of inaccurate, incomplete, or outdated personal data. Controllers must verify and update the records immediately.
Data subjects may request deletion of their data once the purpose of processing is achieved, consent is withdrawn, or processing is unlawful.
Subjects have the right to receive their data in a structured, commonly used formatting and transmit it to another controller.
