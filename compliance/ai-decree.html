Soft Law, Hard Consequences

While Circular No. 9/2023 serves as an ethical guideline, non-compliance signals high risk for future regulation. Axiara ensures your AI deployment respects Inclusivity, Humanity, and Security before they become binding obligations.

The 3 Pillars of Ethical AI

Inclusivity (Keadilan)

AI must not discriminate against any individual or group. We audit training data for hidden biases to ensure fair outcomes for all demographics.

  • Bias Detection
  • Fairness Metrics

Humanity (Kemanusiaan)

AI must protect human dignity and rights. We implement HITL (Human-in-the-Loop) workflows to ensure oversight on critical automated decisions.

  • Human Oversight
  • Explainability (XAI)

Security (Keamanan)

AI systems must be robust against attacks. We conduct adversarial simulations to stress-test your models against manipulation and data poisoning.

  • Adversarial Tests
  • Data Privacy
